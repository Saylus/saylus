Marketing is essential for any budding venture. As such, we have carefully and prudentially selected the following marketing strategies to appeal to and reach out to our demographics:

Niche Marketing

The concept of Niche Marketing is delivering specific product at a specific price to a specific customer. Our target audience is limited to women of lower middle to upper middle households. We have identified two specific places for our products where we can sell it to our target audience.

Social Media Marketing

We will be actively using websites like Blogger, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter etc. to promote and market our product. As per the cost analysis the best among them are Google adwords, Quora, Instagram, Twitter and Text messages. Text messages have become old and can’t be effective in gaining a market for our product.

Trade Show Marketing

We will be involved in marketing products at trade shows, markets and supermarkets. Considering the huge number of customers passing by it, it can be useful. We’ll look for trade shows which specialize in both technology and socially helpful products.

Digital Media Marketing

We will be using Search Engine Optimization initially to make our presence online. Search Engine Optimization will help to make our website’s link flash at the top. Search Engine Optimization is costly but effective because people tend to click on links that are present first. This marketing technique can be used in the long run to promote our product.

Commercials

Commercials are also very expensive particularly for a start-up to bear. If we go for a proper advertisement with a good production house and actors we may have to spend up to ₹ 10Lakhs. If we go for a medium range production house we will have to spend ₹ 5Lakhs which is also expensive for our product. The most efficient option at present that we have is shooting our advertisement ourselves which can be effective. This would cost us around ₹ 5000 only.

Review Marketing

We will offer our product to Bloggers and You Tubers who can review them and post them online. Review marketing is cheap and can be effective for a start up. We can use it to influence and persuade people to buy our product.

Undercover/Buzz Marketing

This includes creating hype or increasing curiosity of the product in the market that attracts customers fast. Analyzing the cost of undercover marketing is very difficult. Some notable points under buzz marketing are:

  1. Influencers

    2. We can take help from various journalists and other people who are working for the society to write stories and articles on our product to create a hype for it and make it easy for us to sell. This may be a little costly.

  2. NGO

    3. This work can also be done by NGOs working towards women empowerment. These NGOs can help us in selling and promoting our product. This wouldn’t be costly as well.



We have also analyzed various other marketing techniques like cross marketing etc. but did not find them feasible and viable for our company.
